# STRATEGY add-on: Who will watch (hypotheses)

Private note for Seb. Paste into **STRATEGY - Chasing Chet** (the untamed private doc).  
Not for the shared production folder. Updated 2026-07-21.

Honest starting point: we do not know yet. The film is not shot. What follows is who *could* watch, so website, ads, and Robin conversations stay aimed at real people rather than "everyone who likes jazz."

---

## 1. Primary: people already in the music

| Who | Why they watch | What they want |
|-----|----------------|----------------|
| UK / EU jazz listeners (clubs, festivals, Radio 3 / Jazz FM types) | Chet is still a live name on bandstands | Story + music, not celebrity gossip |
| Robin's existing audience (gigs, repmusic list, Cambridge/London circuit) | Trust the messenger; many already saw the Chet show with James | Behind the trip, proof the Europe story is real |
| Chet heads who know *Let's Get Lost* | They want the half Weber did not finish | Europe, late albums, collaborators still alive |
| Musicians / students of the trumpet book | Catalog as craft | Top 30, places, how the records were made |

These are the people most likely to **finish** a 75-90 min film or a screening with a live set after.

---

## 2. Secondary: story over genre

| Who | Why they watch | Risk |
|-----|----------------|------|
| Viewers of music docs (*Ronnie's*, *Blue Note*, In-Edit crowd) | Format-ready | Expect polish and clear music |
| European culture / ARTE-ish audience | Europe-made film about European Chet | Gatekeepers, not YouTube first |
| People drawn to addiction + art without wanting a lecture | Sally Marlow lane; Baker's choices | Easy to get moralising or lurid |
| Families of the interviewees / local scenes (Rome, Brussels, NL) | Their person is in it | Small numbers, high loyalty |

---

## 3. Gatekeepers (not "fans," but they decide who else watches)

- Music-doc festivals: In-Edit, Doc'n Roll, Sheffield DocFest, IDFA music strands  
- Broadcasters: ARTE, Sky Arts, NPO, RAI (realistic ceiling from strategy)  
- Jazz venues and festivals that book film + live sets  

Treat these as a **distribution audience**. The public site should not sound like a festival application, but the material we shoot must satisfy them later.

---

## 4. Who is not the audience (do not build the site for them)

- Netflix / streamer "next true crime" crowds  
- People who only want the 1950s pretty Chet and no Europe argument  
- General "jazz is background coffee" playlists with no interest in story  
- Anyone who needs a trailer of *this* film before they click (we do not have one yet)

---

## 5. How each product finds them

| Product | Best audience | When |
|---------|---------------|------|
| Feature | Festival + screening + later broadcast/self | After edit; premiere rules |
| Top 30 series | Chet heads + Robin's channel | From autumn; discovery engine |
| Trip shorts (Green tier only) | Casual social; list signups | During/after trip |
| Event screening + live set | Robin's real strength: local + venue | Path B |

Mailing list is the only asset that serves all of these.

---

## 6. Website implications (for the public page)

Target visitor on first load:

1. Chet-curious or *Let's Get Lost* viewer who wants the Europe half  
2. Someone who already follows Robin / the Chet show  
3. Press / interviewee checking the project is real  

Not: festival programmer (they get a deck later), not Ads tourist promised a stream.

**Too much on the site?** Yes, slightly. The long *Let's Get Lost* block and dual YouTube embeds compete with our own film. Trim to: thesis, short counterpart line, journey, voices, music, credits, email. One outbound video link is enough until we have our own trailer.

**Google Ads later (if any):** target jazz + Chet + music documentary keywords and geo (UK + NL + BE + IT + FR). Landing page must match the ad promise. Do not buy "Chet Baker documentary watch free" until there is something to watch.
